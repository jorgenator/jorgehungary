My admission to Big Battery

On December 27th 2022, I started as an Intern Engineer at Big Battery Panama. The company has just started and I was one of the two first Interns that were picked out to help the company grow.

Big Battery is a cost-effective energy solutions company which purpose is to promote mass adoption of renewable energy or also known as Green Energy. The last mentioned is important for the environment as it replaces the negative effects of fossil fuels with more environmentally-friendly alternatives.

Being part of this company has allowed me to appreciate firsthand the potential that renewable energy holds and see how close we are to seeing change in our country thanks to companies like Big Battery, which is interested in helping other countries join the green energy revolution.
